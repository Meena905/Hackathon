Emergency Help

If you are in immediate danger, please call the helpline below for assistance.

Call National Helpline

Steps to Take in an Emergency:

  1. Call the national helpline immediately for professional assistance.
  2. Contact trusted friends or family for support.
  3. Document any incidents if it is safe to do so.
  4. Seek shelter at the nearest support center if necessary.
