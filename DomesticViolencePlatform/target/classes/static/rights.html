Know Your Rights

1. The Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2005

This law provides protection for women who are victims of domestic violence in India. It ensures that victims have access to shelters, protection orders, and financial support.

2. Section 498A of the Indian Penal Code

This section criminalizes harassment and cruelty towards a married woman by her husband or his relatives, particularly for dowry demands.

3. Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition, and Redressal) Act, 2013

This act ensures protection for women against sexual harassment in the workplace and provides a mechanism to address complaints.
